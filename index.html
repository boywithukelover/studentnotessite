üìò Biology - Cell Structure

Cells are the basic unit of life. Every living organism is made of cells. There are two main types:

  • Prokaryotic (no nucleus)
  • Eukaryotic (has nucleus)
Inside a cell, we have organelles like: Mitochondria (powerhouse), Ribosomes (make proteins), and Cell membrane (controls what goes in/out).

üìó Math - Trigonometry Basics

Trigonometry is all about triangles! Use the formula: SOH CAH TOA

  • Sine = Opposite / Hypotenuse
  • Cosine = Adjacent / Hypotenuse
  • Tangent = Opposite / Adjacent
Practice with right-angled triangles and learn to use sin, cos, and tan.

üìô History - French Revolution

The French Revolution (1789) was a time of major change in France. People protested against the monarchy and unfair taxes. Important points:

  • Storming of the Bastille
  • Declaration of Rights of Man
  • Rise of Napoleon
It influenced revolutions all around the world!
